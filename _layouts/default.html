Hi there! I am Yuchen Lin, a third-year undergraduate student at Peking University, majoring in Artificial Intelligence in Zhi Class (an honors program directed by Prof. Baoquan Chen) of EECS School. I am also double-majoring in Economics at National School of Development. I am honored to be advised by Prof. Yadong Mu in Wangxuan Institute of Computer Technology, Peking University. I have spent a wonderful semester at School of Computer Science of Carnegie Mellon University as a visiting student. Since then, I have been working closely with Prof. Katerina Fragkiadaki in Machine Learning Department, Carnegie Mellon University.
